How to Play Tap Hear

  • Find a quiet place for listening.
  • Decide how long your listening session should be (15 minutes seems nice).
  • When you hear a sound, enter it in the "type here" box and tap/click the "tap here" button (or press the return key on your keyboard). This will add the word to the word cloud below.
  • If you hear the same/similar sound, you can enter it again in the "type here" box or simply tap the word in the word cloud. Either way, the word will get bigger to indicate you've heard it more.
  • Repeat until done.
Click the 'X' above to close this menu when you're ready to get started.
